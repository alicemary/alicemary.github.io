Work Philosophy

I believe in walking the viewer through their experience every step of the way. First by determining the key message that needs to be delivered and then ensuring all content leads to that goal.

An essential part of my workflow is gathering user data to improve design and usability, ultimately arriving at a result that satisfies both my clients and their audience.

During my time researching the cell signalling pathways driving fibrotic lung disease at the Institute for Respiratory Health, I developed an understanding of how important communication is when sharing dense subject matter.

Now as a web designer and illustrator, I work periodically with members of the Faculty of Dentistry, University of Toronto to inform potential students and other interested parties of their research, activities and goals.

I am committed to upholding and exceeding the impeccably high standards of the medical illustration industry and am particularly interested in contributing to the community so that together we can stay on the cutting edge of new technology.
